Common Questions about Drinks

Q: What's the difference between coffee and espresso?

A: Coffee is typically brewed by passing hot water through ground coffee beans, while espresso is made by forcing a small amount of nearly boiling water through finely-ground coffee beans. Espresso is more concentrated and has a layer of crema on top.

Q: What are the health benefits of green tea?

A: Green tea is rich in antioxidants called catechins, which may help prevent cell damage, reduce inflammation, and even fight certain types of cancer. It may also boost metabolism and improve brain function.

Q: What makes a cocktail a "sour"?

A: A sour cocktail typically consists of a base liquor, lemon or lime juice, and a sweetener. Classic examples include the Whiskey Sour and Margarita.

Types of Coffee in Malaysia

  • Kopi: Black coffee with condensed milk.
  • Kopi C: Coffee with evaporated milk and sugar.
  • Kopi C Kosong: Coffee with evaporated milk, no sugar.
  • Kopi O: Black coffee with sugar.
  • Kopi O Kosong: Black coffee without sugar.

Interesting Drink Facts

  • The world's most expensive coffee, Kopi Luwak, comes from coffee beans eaten and partially digested by the Asian palm civet.
  • Bubble tea, also known as boba tea, originated in Taiwan in the 1980s.
  • The oldest known alcoholic beverage is a 9,000-year-old Chinese concoction made from rice, honey, and fruit.
